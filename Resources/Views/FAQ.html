Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start?

A: To start the scheduling process you'll need to log in with your registered information and the given username. Once you're on the course selection page you can select your desired courses. Once you're done with selecting your courses you can then navigate to the scheduling page and then you can drag the classes onto an open space of the schedule. The schedule will also show if there are any class time interferences due to reasons such as a dual credit class or a steam class. Once you have created your desired schedule you'll be able to click the finish button and then either download or print out a copy of your schedule on the final page.

How can I save my schedule?

A: Once you have completed your desired schedule you'll be able to print or save the schedule by going to the final page.

How to access Dark mode?

A: To access dark mode you can simply press the little moon on the navigation bar and then you can press the sun to change it back to light mode.

Is any of my information going to be stored?

A: Once you have login into the scheduler with your information none of it will be stored for safety purposes.

How many schedules should I make?

A: Usually we'll recommend a minimum of 3 schedules since it's not always guaranteed that you'll get the desired schedule

Why should I use this?

A: You can use this scheduler to create an ideal schedule that can prepare you for the arena scheduling.
