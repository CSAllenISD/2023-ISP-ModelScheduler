Frequently Asked Questions:

How can I save my schedule?

Once you have created your desired schedule you'll be able to print out or download the schedule on your computer usually located on the top right of the final page of the scheduler.

Is any of my information going to be stored?

Once you have login into the scheduler with your information none of it will be stored for safty purposes.

How many schedules should I make

Usually we'll recommend a minimum of 3 schedules since it's not always guaranteed that you'll get the desired schedule
