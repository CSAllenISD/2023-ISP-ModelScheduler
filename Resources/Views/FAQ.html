Frequently Asked Questions:

How do I start?

To start the scheduling process you'll need to login with your registered information and once your in the scheduling page you'll be able to select the avaliable classes and drag it into an open space of the schedule. The schedule will also show if there is any class time interfernces due to reasons such as a duel credit class or a steam class. Once you have created your desired schedule you'll be able to click the finish button and then either download or print out a copy of your schedule.

How can I save my schedule?

Once you have created your desired schedule you'll be able to print out or download the schedule on your computer usually located on the top right of the final page of the scheduler.

Is any of my information going to be stored?

Once you have login into the scheduler with your information none of it will be stored for safty purposes.

How many schedules should I make

Usually we'll recommend a minimum of 3 schedules since it's not always guaranteed that you'll get the desired schedule
