    What is the demand calculator?
    The demand calculator shows relatively how full the class you are trying to take is at a certain period. It is calculated using the total users on the website and the total users scheduling the same class at the same time.

    Is any of my information going to be stored?
    Once you have logged into the scheduler with your information none of it will be stored for safety purposes.
    How many schedules should I make?
    Usually we recommend a minimum of 3 schedules, as it is not always guaranteed that you will get the desired schedule.
    What do I do after I'm done with the schedule?
    Once you have finalized your schedule, you can print it out or just leave it as is.
